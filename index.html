Wearable Health Insights

Utilizing wearable device data to understand and mitigate the risk of miscarriage.

About Our Project

This project focuses on analyzing real-time health data collected from wearable devices to identify potential risk factors for miscarriage. By integrating data such as heart rate, stress levels, sleep patterns, and physical activity, we aim to develop predictive models to assist healthcare providers in delivering personalized care.

Data We Use

  • Heart Rate Variability
  • Stress Levels
  • Physical Activity (Walking, Sitting, etc.)
  • Body Temperature
  • Sleep Quality and Duration
  • Blood Glucose Levels

Privacy and Compliance

We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of user data. All data collected is anonymized and used strictly for research purposes in accordance with GDPR and HIPAA guidelines.
